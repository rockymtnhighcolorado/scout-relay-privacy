# Privacy Policy for NHL Live

**Last Updated: March 11, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how NHL Live ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the App. NHL Live is a companion application for Discord-based sports bots, providing real-time game event updates via a relay connection.

By using NHL Live, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

NHL Live collects and processes a minimal amount of information, limited to what is necessary for the App to function:

- **Authentication Token**: A one-time token provided to you by a Discord bot owner. This token contains a username and team identifier used to establish your relay session.
- **Username**: Extracted from your authentication token and used only to identify your active session on the relay server.
- **WebSocket Connection Data**: Basic connection metadata (such as connection timestamps) required to maintain the real-time relay session between the App and the relay server.

### Information We Do Not Collect

NHL Live does **not** collect, access, or process any of the following:

- Personal information such as your real name, email address, phone number, or mailing address
- Location or GPS data
- Contacts, photos, or media files
- Microphone or camera input
- Device identifiers for advertising or tracking purposes
- Browsing history or usage analytics
- Financial or payment information

The App does not use any analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

## How We Use Your Information

The limited information described above is used solely to:

- Authenticate your session with the relay server
- Maintain an active WebSocket connection so you can receive real-time game events
- Display your username within the relay session

We do not use your information for marketing, profiling, advertising, or any purpose beyond delivering the core functionality of the App.

## Data Retention

- **Authentication tokens** expire within 24 hours of issuance and are not stored on any server beyond their validity period.
- **Session data** (username and connection metadata) exists only for the duration of your active WebSocket connection and is discarded when you disconnect.
- No personal data is retained on our servers after your token expires or your session ends.

## Data Storage and Security

Session data is processed on a relay server maintained by the Developer. We use WebSocket Secure (WSS) connections to protect data in transit. Because no personal data is persisted beyond the active session, there is no long-term data store containing user information.

## Third-Party Services

NHL Live does not share any user data with third parties. The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The App communicates exclusively with the Developer's relay server to provide its core functionality. No data is transmitted to any other external service.

## Account Creation

NHL Live does not require or support account creation. Access is granted through one-time authentication tokens generated and distributed by Discord bot owners. The Developer does not manage user accounts or credentials.

## Children's Privacy

NHL Live is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because the App does not collect personal information and access requires a token provided by a Discord bot owner, there is no mechanism through which a child could independently provide personal data to us.

If you believe a child under 13 has somehow provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the email address below, and we will take steps to address the concern.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Because NHL Live does not store personal data beyond active sessions, there is generally no persistent data to access, modify, or delete. If you have questions about your data or wish to exercise any applicable rights, please contact us using the information below.
